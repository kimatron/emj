Hey there! I'm Emilija,

A Professional Photographer (with an unprofessional sense of humor)

My approach combines technical precision with artistic intuition, allowing me to capture authentic moments that tell compelling stories. I'm like that friend who always notices the tiny details – except I have a professional camera and won't bombard you with 47 consecutive selfies.

Whether I'm shooting on location or battling with lighting setups in someone's living room (who needs a studio when the world is your backdrop?), I prioritize connection—with my subjects, with light, and with the environment—to create images that resonate. And yes, I'll make you look good. That's kind of the whole point.

"Photography is the art of freezing moments that would otherwise melt away into the puddle of forgotten memories. Also, it's the only profession where 'shooting people' is perfectly acceptable in polite conversation."

01. Services Offered

I offer a range of photography services. Each service is tailored to your specific requirements, ensuring results that make both of us look good.

Portrait Photography

Capturing your unique personality without the awkward "cheese" smiles. Whether it's individuals, families, or corporate headshots that don't scream "I hate this."

Wedding Photography

Documenting your special day with attention to detail, candid moments, and the exact second your best man realizes he forgot his speech.

Commercial Photography

Product and brand photography that makes your stuff look so good, people will wonder if it's Photoshopped. (It's not. I'm just that good.)

Location Scouting

I'll find the perfect backdrop for your shoot, whether it's urban grit, rolling countryside, or that one wall that matches your Instagram aesthetic.

Event Photography

Coverage of parties, concerts, and corporate events. I'll capture all the networking magic and that one colleague who had one too many.

Fashion Photography

Creative fashion shots to make anyone look like they just stepped off a runway (even if you normally live in sweatpants—no judgment here).

02. My Gear

While the photographer makes the image (not the gear), having the right tools helps. Here's my trusty equipment arsenal that accompanies me on every adventure. Yes, I name my cameras. No, I won't apologize for it.

Primary Camera

Sony Alpha a7 III (aka "Bertha") - Full-frame mirrorless that's survived rain, weddings, and being dropped exactly once (we don't talk about it).

Lenses

My holy trinity: 24-70mm f/2.8 for versatility, 85mm f/1.4 for portraits that make people gasp, and a 35mm f/1.8 that's with me even on grocery runs.

Lighting

Two Godox flashes that have illuminated everything from corporate events to impromptu dance parties, plus a collection of light modifiers that make people look good even on Monday mornings.

Extras

A steady tripod, colorful backdrops, and enough memory cards to document the entire population of a small country. Also, snacks. Lots of snacks.
